1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

1.1. Personal Information

  • Name, Email Address, and Phone Number: We collect this information when you register or interact with the app for personalized services, communication, and support.
  • Contacts: We collect contact information from your device to enable features such as sharing, synchronization, or recommendations. We do not store or share your contacts without your consent.
  • Calendar: We access your calendar to schedule meetings, set reminders, and provide notifications for your events.
  • SMS: We access SMS to read one-time passwords (OTP) for seamless authentication and account verification purposes.
  • Device ID: We collect your device ID to enhance your experience with CubeOne App. This information helps us identify your device, maintain security, and improve service functionality.
  • Precise Location Data: With your consent, we collect and use your device’s precise location data to provide location-based services such as navigation and nearby recommendations. This data is used solely to enhance your user experience and is not shared with third parties without your permission.
  • Camera and Microphone: We access your camera and microphone for features like video conferencing, taking photos, or recording videos. We do not record or store any media without your consent.
  • Storage Access: We access your device storage to save photos, videos, and documents created within the app.
  • Bluetooth: Bluetooth permissions are used for connecting to Bluetooth devices, such as speakers, headsets, or other compatible devices.
  • System Alerts: We may display system alerts for notifications and updates that require your immediate attention.
  • Biometric Data: CubeOne may collect biometric data, such as fingerprint or face recognition, for enhanced security and app access control.

1.2. Non-Personal Information

  • We may collect non-personal information about Users whenever they interact with the App. This may include technical information about Users' devices, such as the device type, operating system, and internet connection.
  • App Interactions: Data related to app usage and interactions is collected to improve our services, identify usage patterns, and enhance user experience.
  • Crash Logs: Collected to identify and resolve technical issues, enhance app stability, and improve performance.

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
