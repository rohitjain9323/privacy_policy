Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how CubeOne App ("we," "us," or "our"), managed by Futurescape Technologies Pvt. Ltd., collects, uses, and discloses your information. By using our app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal information to provide and improve our services.

  • Personal Information: Includes your name, email, phone number, address, payment information, and any other data you provide directly to us.
  • Non-Personal Information: Includes device information, log data, and analytics.
  • Text Message Data: We collect SMS messages for OTP verification and other user authentication purposes. This data is processed locally on your device and not shared with third parties.
  • Purchase History: We collect information about your purchase history within the app to provide personalized recommendations and improve our services.
  • Address Data: We collect your address data for purposes such as deliveries, personalized recommendations, and enhancing location-based features.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Process transactions and send related information, including purchase confirmations.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Prevent fraud, protect our rights, and ensure the security of our services.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our app and providing services to you, but only under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Once your data is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted.

6. Collection of Text Message Data

Our app may collect text message data, including SMS, for purposes such as OTP (One-Time Password) verification to enhance security during user authentication. This data is processed locally on your device and is not stored or shared with third parties.

7. Collection of Purchase History

We may collect information related to your purchase history to enhance your experience within the app, such as providing personalized recommendations or improving our service offerings. This data is kept secure and is not sold or shared with unauthorized parties.

8. Collection of Address Data

We collect address data to facilitate services such as order deliveries, location-based features, and personalized recommendations. Address data is handled securely and used solely to enhance your experience with our app.

9. Your Rights to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe that your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your country. We encourage you to contact us first to resolve any issues, but you may also contact your local data protection authority.

10. Rights Under GDPR (for EU Users)

As an EU resident, you have specific rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • The right to access your personal data.
  • The right to correct inaccuracies in your data.
  • The right to request the deletion of your data.
  • The right to restrict or object to the processing of your data.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

11. Rights Under LGPD (for Users in Brazil)

If you are a resident of Brazil, you have rights under the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD), including:

  • The right to confirm the existence of data processing.
  • The right to access your data.
  • The right to correct incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data.
  • The right to anonymize, block, or delete unnecessary or excessive data.
  • The right to data portability.
  • The right to know with whom your data has been shared.
  • The right to revoke consent at any time.

12. Rights Under CCPA (for California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data is being collected about you.
  • Request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal data.
  • Receive equal service and pricing, even if you exercise your privacy rights.
  • Know whether your data is being shared with third parties and to whom.

To exercise your rights under CCPA, please contact us at dpo@futurescapetech.com or use the in-app settings to manage your privacy preferences.

13. Rights Under VCDPA (for Virginia Residents)

If you are a Virginia resident, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and obtain a copy of your personal data. You also have the right to opt out of the sale of your data, targeted advertising, and profiling for decisions with legal or significant effects.

14. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: You have given explicit consent for processing your data for specific purposes.
  • Contractual Necessity: Processing is necessary for the performance of a contract with you.
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing is necessary for our legitimate interests, such as improving our services.
  • Legal Compliance: Processing is necessary to comply with our legal obligations.

15. Opt-Out of Data Sharing

To opt-out of data sharing, targeted advertising, or any other use of your personal data, please contact us at support@futurescapetech.com. You may also adjust your device or app settings to limit data collection or withdraw your consent at any time.

16. Permissions and APIs Access

Our app requests permissions and accesses APIs only when necessary to implement features or services that are critical to your experience within the app. Permissions are requested incrementally and in context so that you understand why we need access. We do not use or request access to sensitive data for purposes that are not disclosed or for features that are not implemented.

We use the data only for purposes that you have consented to. If, in the future, we need to use your data for other purposes, we will ask for your explicit consent and ensure you agree to the additional uses.

17. Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to third-party services. This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party services, and we are not responsible for the privacy practices of these services. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before engaging with them.

18. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
